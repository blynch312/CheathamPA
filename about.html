Who Are We?

Cheatham and Associates, P.A., established in 1981, is a professional corporation registered in North Carolina specializing in Mechnical, Electrical, Plumbing and Fire Protection Engineering and Design. During our 35 year history in coastal North Carolina, Cheatham and Associates, P.A. has successfully completed over 2,500 projects for municipalities, state, federal, institutional, commercial, and industrial clients. We strive to have direct involvement with the owner and owner’s representative for an in-depth understanding of our client’s specific goals and expectations for each individual project.

Cheatham and Associates has provided mechanical, electrical, plumbing and fire protection design services. These services include HVAC renovations/replacement including chillers and boilers, kitchen hood systems and utility chases, and energy modeling. Electrical services include fire alarm systems, security, audio/visual, communications, interior/exterior lighting and switchgear, and energy modeling. Plumbing services include renovations/replacement including cold and hot water piping systems, domestic water heating, fixtures, and water construction designs. Fire Protection design includes load calculations and designs for fire sprinkler.

We work to execute projects with efficiency and professionalism from concept and design development through project construction, completion and final closeouts. We offer a full range of construction administration capabilities from minor involvement to a full scope as prime consultant. Our firm’s experience in planning, design and construction administration, combined with our client-focused approach, has earned us a reputation of superior service and integrity.
